About Us

For more than 25 years, Starlight Hospital has served the community with a mission to improve health and offer compassionate, high‑quality, and cost‑effective healthcare. Our state‑of‑the‑art infrastructure, cutting‑edge technology, and dedicated medical professionals ensure that every patient receives the best care possible.

Key Departments

Cardiology

Comprehensive cardiac care including preventive cardiology, interventional procedures, and rehabilitation.

Orthopedics

Advanced treatment for bone and joint conditions, minimally invasive surgeries, and sports medicine.

Neurology

Expert diagnosis and management of brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care with multidisciplinary tumor boards and cutting‑edge therapies.

Pediatrics

Dedicated child‑friendly care including neonatology, immunizations, and developmental services.

24×7 Emergency

Round‑the‑clock emergency room supported by critical care and trauma teams.
